Choosing Your First Deck

Starter decks are recommended for beginners because they are balanced and easy to learn.

Playing Online

New players can learn using Magic Arena, a digital version of the game that teaches rules through tutorials and guided matches.

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with simple cards.
  • Focus on learning the rules first.
  • Play casual games while practicing.
  • Ask experienced players for help.
