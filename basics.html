Card Types

Magic cards fall into several main types, including creatures, lands, and spells. Creatures attack opponents and defend players. Lands provide mana, which is used to play cards. Spells create special effects that help players gain advantages.

Mana and Resources

Mana is the energy used to cast spells and summon creatures. Players gain mana by playing land cards each turn.

Turn Structure

A typical turn includes drawing a card, playing a land, casting spells, and attacking with creatures.
